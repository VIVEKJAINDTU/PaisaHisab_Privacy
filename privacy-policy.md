# Privacy Policy for PaisaHisab

**Last Updated: December 23, 2024**

This Privacy Policy describes how PaisaHisab ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Data You Provide
When you use PaisaHisab, you may provide the following information:
- **Expense Information:** Amount, category, merchant name, date, and notes you enter manually
- **Budget Information:** Budget limits, categories, and periods you set
- **Recurring Expenses:** Information about recurring expenses you configure

### 1.2 SMS Messages
With your explicit permission, PaisaHisab accesses SMS messages on your device to automatically detect and record expense transactions from bank notifications. This feature:
- Only processes SMS messages that appear to be transaction notifications
- Processes SMS data entirely on your device - no SMS content is transmitted to external servers
- Only extracts transaction amount, merchant name, and date - no other SMS content is stored
- Can be disabled at any time through app settings

### 1.3 Automatically Collected Information
To improve app performance and user experience, we collect:
- **Analytics Data:** App usage statistics, feature usage, and performance metrics through Firebase Analytics
- **Crash Reports:** Error logs and crash information through Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs and improve stability
- **Device Information:** Device model, Android version, and app version (for technical support purposes)

**Note:** Analytics and crash data do not include your personal expense information, SMS content, or any financial data.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- **Expense Tracking:** To store and display your expense records, budgets, and spending analytics
- **Automatic Detection:** To automatically detect and record expenses from SMS notifications (with your permission)
- **Budget Management:** To track your spending against budgets and send budget alerts
- **App Improvement:** To analyze app usage patterns and fix bugs through crash reports
- **Notifications:** To send you budget alerts and spending summaries (you can disable these in settings)

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Local Storage
**All your expense and budget data is stored locally on your device.** This includes:
- All expense records
- Budget information
- Recurring expense configurations
- App preferences and settings

Your financial data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

### 3.2 Data Security
We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your data:
- Data is stored in an encrypted local database on your device
- No cloud synchronization or external data transmission
- SMS processing occurs entirely on-device

## 4. Third-Party Services

### 4.1 Firebase Services
We use Google Firebase services for:
- **Firebase Analytics:** To understand how users interact with the app (anonymized usage statistics)
- **Firebase Crashlytics:** To collect crash reports and error logs to improve app stability

Firebase services are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. Analytics and crash data are anonymized and do not include your personal financial information.

### 4.2 Advertising ID
PaisaHisab uses advertising ID solely for analytics purposes through Firebase Analytics. We do not use advertising ID for advertising or marketing purposes, and we do not share it with advertising partners.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not sell, trade, or share your personal expense or financial data with third parties.**

The only data transmitted externally is:
- Anonymized analytics data to Firebase Analytics (usage statistics, not financial data)
- Crash reports to Firebase Crashlytics (error logs, not financial data)

We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:
- **Access:** You can view all your data within the app
- **Export:** You can export your expense data to CSV or JSON format at any time
- **Delete:** You can delete individual expenses, budgets, or all data by uninstalling the app
- **SMS Permission:** You can revoke SMS permission at any time through device settings
- **Notifications:** You can disable budget alerts and spending summaries in app settings
- **Analytics:** You can opt out of analytics data collection (though this may affect our ability to improve the app)

## 7. Children's Privacy

PaisaHisab is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## 8. Data Retention

Your expense and budget data is retained on your device until you:
- Manually delete it within the app
- Uninstall the app (which removes all local data)
- Clear app data through device settings

Analytics and crash data are retained by Firebase according to their retention policies (typically 14 months for analytics).

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- For significant changes, we may provide additional notification

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## 10. Permissions Used

PaisaHisab requests the following permissions:
- **READ_SMS / RECEIVE_SMS:** To automatically detect expense transactions from bank SMS notifications (optional, can be disabled)
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS:** To send budget alerts and spending summary notifications (can be disabled in settings)
- **READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE:** To export expense data to CSV/JSON files (only when you choose to export)

All permissions are optional and can be revoked at any time through device settings.
